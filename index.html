Our Mission

At Squirrel Savings Bank, we believe financial literacy should be fun, accessible, and inspiring. Since 2012, we've helped thousands of children, families, and young adults develop smart money habits by following nature's example - just like squirrels prepare for winter, we help people prepare for their financial futures.
